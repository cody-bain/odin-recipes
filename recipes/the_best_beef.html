The Best Beef

Recipe for The Best Beef

Simply put, this is the best chicken you'll ever eat. Cooking time is ~ 15 minutes and cleap-up is minimal.

After one taste, you'll be begging for more.

Ingredients

  • 1lb 92/8 Ground Beef
  • 1 Tbsp Salt
  • 1 Tbsp Pepper
  • 1 metric ton of Red Hot Sauce

Steps

  1. Dump your beef in the skillet. Season with salt and pepper
  2. Once browned, you're done.
  3. Plate the beef & coat it in Red Hot.
  4. Ponder why we haven't made this dish the 8th wonder of the world.
Bon Appetit.
