The Best Eggs

Recipe for The Best Eggs

Simply put, these are the best eggs you'll ever eat. Cooking time is < 10 minutes and cleap-up is minimal.

After one taste, you'll be begging for more.

Ingredients

  • 4 Eggs
  • 1 Tbsp Butter
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Pepper
  • 1 metric ton of salsa

Steps

  1. Melt your butter on a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Once the butter has melted, crack 4 eggs into the skillet.
  3. Wait until the bottom has cooked, then give her a flip. No spatula needed. Just a "flick a da wrist".
  4. Turn off heat & let eggs sit for 1 minute.
  5. Remove eggs from skillet, top with salt and pepper.
  6. Now drench your eggs in salsa.
Bon Appetit.
